How Our System Works

Register Your Vehicle

Users register their vehicles in the system, providing essential details and ownership documents.

Report Theft Instantly

In case of theft, the owner can immediately report their vehicle stolen through their dashboard.

AI-Powered Scanning

Our AI continuously scans public surveillance for the stolen vehicle’s license plate and features.

What It Can Provide

Instant Alerts

Receive instant notifications when your stolen vehicle is detected by any connected camera system.

Secure System

All user data and vehicle records are securely stored and encrypted to prevent unauthorized access.

Live Tracking

If detected, the vehicle’s location is shared in real-time with law enforcement and the owner.
